Ingredients

  • 5 kg Pale Malt
  • 500 g Munich
  • 100 g Citra Hops
  • Yeast: Wyeast 1056
  • Orange Peel

Brewing Instructions

Mash at 67°C for 60 minutes.

Boil for 60 minutes, add hops accordingly.

Cool to 20°C and pitch yeast.

Ferment for 2 weeks at 19°C.

Yeast Starter Instructions

Make a 1.5L starter 2 days prior to brew day.
