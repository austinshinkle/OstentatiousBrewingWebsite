description: "This beer is intended to be a high ABV, but light lager. I am having fun with my second fermenter and this is the chance to experiment a little. I am looking for a clear and light body beer that has a little behind the scenes kick. Something that is light and refreshing but with ABV that sneaks up on you because...why not?"
malts:
    - 6 lbs Great Western Superior Pilsen Malt
    - 5 lbs Great Western Pale Ale Malt
    - 0.5 lbs Carapils Malt
    - 2 lb Corn Sugar
hops:
    - 0.75 oz Hallertau Mittelfrüh Hops (4.5% AA) - 0 minutes
    - 2 oz Hallertau Mittelfrüh Hops (4.5% AA) - 50 minutes
yeast:
    - 1 vial of harvested WLP800 Pilsner Lager Yeast
other_ingredients:
    - 15 drops FermCap
    - 1 tablet Whirlfloc
    - 1/2 tsp Yeast Nutrient
    - 1 campden tablet (crushed)
    - 8.85 gallons Water
brew_date: "2021-02-06"
target_og: "1.070"
actual_og: "1.071"
target_fg: "1.009"
actual_fg: "1.020"
target_abv: "8.0%"
actual_abv: "6.9%"
calculated_ibu: "22"
calculated_srm: "4.3"
cost: "$18.05"
brewing_instructions:
    - Add 8.85 gallons of strike water to the kettle
    - "Add one campden tablet (crushed)"
    - Measure out 5.25 gallons for first infusion
    - Heat 5.25 gallons of strike water to 160°F
    - Add 5.25 gallons of water to the mash tun
    - Add all grains to mash tun and confirm temperature of 148°F
    - "While steeping, heat 3.6 gallons of water to 171°F for lautering"
    - Extract first runnings into boil kettle
    - "Add 3.6 gallons water and wait 10 minutes"
    - Extract second runnings into boil kettle
    - "Total wort volume should be 7.1 gallons"
    - Add 15 drops FermCap to reduce foaming
    - Bring water to a boil and wait for the hot break
    - Begin boil
    - "At 0 minutes, add 0.75 oz Hallertau Mittelfrüh hops"
    - "At 45 minutes, add one tablet Whirlfloc"
    - "At 45 minutes, add 2 lbs corn sugar"
    - "At 50 minutes, add 1/2 tsp yeast nutrient"
    - "At 50 minutes, add 2 oz Hallertau Mittelfrüh hops"
    - "At 60 minutes, chill the wort to approx. 65°F"
    - Aerate with pure oxygen for 1 minute
    - "Move to the primary fermenter (5.75 gallons)"
    - Pitch yeast from yeast starter
    - Ferment at 52°F for 14 days
    - "Ferment at 60°F for 4 days (diacetyl rest)"
    - "Lower the temp by 3°F/day until 34°F and lager for 10 days"
    - Move to keg and lager for 2 weeks at 34°F
    - Force carbonate
starter_instructions:
    - Boil 7 oz DME in 3 L of water for 10 minutes
    - Let cool to 68°F
    - Pitch harvested WLP800
    - Start stir plate
